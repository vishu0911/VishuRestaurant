About Vishu Sweets & Restaurant

Founded in 2025 by Happy Sing, Vishu Sweets & Restaurant started as a small shop in the heart of the city with a simple mission: to provide high-quality traditional sweets made with pure ingredients.

We are dedicated to preserving the authentic taste of Indian sweets. We use only pure desi ghee, finest milk, and select nuts in all our preparations, following traditional recipes passed down through generations.

As a family-run business, we treat every customer like a member of our own family. Our commitment to quality and authenticity has made us a trusted name for celebrations, festivals, and everyday indulgences.

Today, while we are new in this journey, we remain true to our roots – ensuring every sweet that leaves our shop carries the love, care, and tradition that has been our hallmark since day one.
